A Promise, Always

Not loud promises. Not perfect ones. Just honest words meant only for you.

I promise to listen when you speak, not just hear you — even when the truth is difficult.

I promise to be patient with your emotions, especially on the days you feel too much or think too deeply.

I promise to communicate instead of disappearing into silence.

I promise to control my anger and never let it become words that hurt you.

I promise that even when I am upset, my respect for you will never change.

I promise to pause, breathe, and choose understanding over reaction.

I promise to reassure you instead of letting you feel unsure.

I promise to protect your trust and treat your openness as something sacred.

I promise to choose effort over ego, always.

I promise to keep choosing you — quietly, sincerely, and with my whole heart.

This is not perfection.
This is intention. This is love.
